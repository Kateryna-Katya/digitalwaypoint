Past Way

The World Ended. Your Story Is Just Beginning.

Look, you're trapped in this zombie hellscape, and I'm not gonna sugarcoat it - one wrong move and you're toast. Scavenge for supplies, uncover dark secrets, and fight your way back to the ones you love.

Features

  • Survive at Any Cost

    Scavenge for ammo, food, and water in a world that wants you dead. Bullets are scarce as hell, so if you go all Rambo on everything, you'll be kicking yourself when you're facing down a horde with an empty clip.

  • A Story That Pulls You In

    Sure, you're trying not to die, but there's this whole mystery you can't shake. Piece by piece, you'll start connecting the dots about how everything went to shit as you progress through the story.

  • Fight for Your Family

    You're not just running from the undead. You have a reason to keep going. That's the only thing that'll stop you from giving up when everything goes sideways.

  • Choices With Consequences

    Your decisions shape the path ahead. There's no perfect choice here - just whatever you can sleep at night with.
